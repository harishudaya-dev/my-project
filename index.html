Best Arts and Science College in Chennai Affiliated to the University of Madras

Sri Muthukumaran Arts and Science College was established in 2001 by the Sri Muthukumaran Educational Trust under the able guidance of Thiru. A. N. Radha Krishnan. Affiliated to the University of Madras, the college is recognized as one of the best Arts and Science Colleges in Chennai. It offers high-quality undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, and Management. With modern classrooms, experienced faculty, strong placement support, and a focus on value-based education, Sri Muthukumaran Arts and Science College stands out as a premier institution for holistic learning and career growth.
